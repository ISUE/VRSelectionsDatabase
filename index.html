Database Entries

Note: Entries with * and ** indicate that the Interactor and Indication or Confirmation values have two variations. For Interactor and Indication, direct grab with virtual hand and pointing with a visible or an invisible ray are active depending on the distance from the target. For Confirmation, Analog - Continued Press is combined with Dwell. For these instances, we used the same video timestamp and differentiate between them with the * and **.
