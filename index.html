Explanation

  • Attributes (features) of context in VR object selection scenarios were collected.
  • This t-SNE visualization was produced by representing the levels of all attributes as numbers, and condensing them down to 2D.
  • Entries in the table below (scroll down) correspond to the points in scatter plot.

Visualizer Instructions

  • Click on individual points to see more information about a scenario
  • Click on the "Open Scatterplot Filters" button to open the filter menu
  • In the opened window, explore the attributes scenario attributes and click on the YouTube logo link that takes you directly to the occurrence of the selection in an online video.

Table Instructions

  • The first time you click on a specific column header, the sorting direction is ascending order. Click again, and the sorting direction will be descending order.
  • For custom filtering, type text into the search box above the table.

Scatterplot Filter Menu
